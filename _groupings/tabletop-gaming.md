## Featured works

- **Dungeon World** — A light and quick roleplaying game where you play dungeon-crawling heroes. (License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0)
- **Fate Accelerated Edition** — An action-focused roleplaying game where you describe a character's key aspects. (License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0)
- **Brigandine** — A skirmish miniatures game focused on simplicity and speed. (License: GNU Free Documentation License)
- **4C System** — A superhero roleplaying game cloned from a popular 1980s RPG. (License: Public domain)
- **Piecepack** — A set of pieces that can be used to play hundreds of different boardgames. (License: Public domain)
- **Mist-Robed Gate** — A roleplaying game of emotionally tempestuous arthaus wuxia. (License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0)
- **The Plant** — A solo game where you play a person wandering the innards of an abandoned power plant looking for your daughter. (License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0)
